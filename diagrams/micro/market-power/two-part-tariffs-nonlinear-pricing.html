Interpretation

Entry fees. A two-part tariff separates the per-unit decision from the participation decision: p controls quantity, while F controls entry.
Surplus extraction. If F = CSi(p), the firm extracts all surplus from type i while leaving that type indifferent to entering.
Screening. A nonlinear menu uses different pairs (Fj,pj) so different types self-select into different plans.
