Interpretation

Graphically. The firm runs a separate monopoly problem in each segment. The common marginal cost line is the only object linking the markets.
Markup logic. The segment with lower absolute elasticity receives the higher Lerner index and therefore the higher markup.
Allocation. Output is allocated so that the marginal revenue in every active segment equals the same marginal cost.

Exam-style summary

1
Separate consumers into segments with no resale/arbitrage.
2
For each segment, write pᵢ(qᵢ) and MRᵢ(qᵢ).
3
Solve MRᵢ(qᵢ)=MC for every active segment.
4
Price from each segment’s demand curve: pᵢ=pᵢ(qᵢ).
5
Check markups: (pᵢ−MC)/pᵢ=1/|εᵢ|. Less elastic groups pay more.
