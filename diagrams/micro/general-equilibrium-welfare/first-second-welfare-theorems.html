First and Second Welfare Theorems

The First Welfare Theorem says that a Walrasian equilibrium is Pareto efficient. The Second Welfare Theorem says that, under convexity and complete markets, efficient allocations can be decentralized by redistributing endowments and then letting prices do the rest.
