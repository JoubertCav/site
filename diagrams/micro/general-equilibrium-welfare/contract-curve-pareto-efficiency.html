It is independent of the initial endowment. Changing \(\omega\) moves the lens and the core, not the full set of Pareto-efficient allocations.

Core allocations

The core is the part of the contract curve that is individually rational relative to \(\omega\).

What to notice

  • If \(MRS_A \neq MRS_B\), the indifference curves cross and local gains from trade remain.
  • The black segment appears only where efficiency and individual rationality hold together.
  • A Pareto weight \(\lambda\) selects one efficient allocation, but \(\omega\) selects which efficient allocations are voluntary.
