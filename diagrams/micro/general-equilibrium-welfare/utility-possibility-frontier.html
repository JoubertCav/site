What to notice

  • The contract curve is drawn in goods space; the UPF is its image in utility space.
  • Changing endowments shifts the disagreement point, so the Nash criterion can move even when the feasible frontier is unchanged.
  • Utilitarian, Rawlsian, and Nash criteria all choose points on the frontier, but they use different supporting geometry.
