What to notice

  • The same line through \(\omega\) is the budget line for both agents.
  • At arbitrary prices, A's demand and B's demand usually identify two different points in the box.
  • At \(p^*\), the two demand points coincide: individual optimization becomes collective feasibility.

Exam-style summary

A Walrasian equilibrium is a price vector and feasible allocation such that every consumer maximizes utility subject to wealth generated by the endowment.
