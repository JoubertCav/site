Interpretation
Centering.
Subtract \(E[S_n]\) to remove deterministic drift.
Scaling.
Divide by \(s_n=\sqrt{Var(S_n)}\) so the limit has variance 1.
Asymptotic normality.
The standardized law approaches \(N(0,1)\), not the raw sum.
Lindeberg.
The Gaussian limit needs no single summand to carry a non-negligible part of the scaled shock.
