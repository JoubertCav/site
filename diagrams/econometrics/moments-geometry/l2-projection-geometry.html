Interpretation
Projection theorem.
For a closed subspace \(\mathcal M\subset L^2\), there is a unique \(\widehat Y\in\mathcal M\) minimizing \(E[(Y-M)^2]\).
Orthogonality.
The error \(e=Y-\widehat Y\) is orthogonal to every basis element used in the predictor.
Best linear prediction.
When \(\mathcal M=\mathrm{span}\{1,X\}\), \(\widehat Y=\alpha+\beta X\).
Normal equations.
Regression first-order conditions are exactly \(\langle e,1\rangle=0\) and \(\langle e,X\rangle=0\).
