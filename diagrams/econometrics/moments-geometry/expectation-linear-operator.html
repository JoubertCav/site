Interpretation
Expectation takes a random function and keeps only its constant component. The remaining part is risk/noise with mean zero.
Operator.
\(E\) maps a whole function \(X(Ï‰)\) into one scalar.
Projection intuition.
\(E[X]\mathbf 1\) is the constant shadow of \(X\).
Linearity.
The shadow of \(aX+bY\) equals \(a\) times the shadow of \(X\) plus \(b\) times the shadow of \(Y\).
Jensen.
Nonlinear curvature does not commute with averaging.
What to notice
Linearity concerns the projection itself. Jensen concerns what happens when you bend the variable before projecting.
