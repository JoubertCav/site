Skewness: exact signed decomposition
\(\gamma_1 = L + R\), where \(L=\int_{z<0}z^3f_Z\), \(R=\int_{z>0}z^3f_Z\)
Kurtosis: exact integral plus auxiliary tail partition
\(\kappa=\int z^4f_Z\); center/tail split is only a visual diagnostic
Interpretation
Skewness.
The left half contributes negatively because \(z^3<0\), and the right half contributes positively because \(z^3>0\).
Symmetry.
If \(f_Z(z)=f_Z(-z)\), the signed cubic areas cancel exactly, so \(\gamma_1=0\).
Kurtosis.
Since \(z^4\ge0\), left and right tails do not cancel. Extremes reinforce each other.
Excess kurtosis.
\(\kappa-3\) compares fourth-moment geometry with the standard normal benchmark.
