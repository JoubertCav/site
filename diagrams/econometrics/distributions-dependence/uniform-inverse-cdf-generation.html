What to notice

  • Uniform probability is distance on [0,1].
  • The CDF converts an observation into its rank: F(X).
  • The inverse CDF converts a rank into an observation: F⁻¹(U).
  • Where the density is small, the quantile map expands intervals.

Compact exam summary

Conceptual distinction

A pseudo-random generator usually produces numbers that behave like independent uniforms. Probability theory then supplies deterministic transformations. Simulation is therefore not a new law-making device; it is a transport map from [0,1] to the desired sample space.
