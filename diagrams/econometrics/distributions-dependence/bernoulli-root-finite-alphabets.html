Probability mass on a count lattice

A binomial variable is a collapsed binary sequence: only the number of successes remains.

theory simulation

Changing \(p\) moves probability mass toward success; changing \(n\) refines the count lattice and makes the mass concentrate around \(np\).

Parameter geometry

Binary laws live on a line segment; finite alphabets live on a simplex.

conceptual reading

The Bernoulli law is the root object. The binomial law is obtained by summing independent Bernoulli indicators.
