Conjugacy and canonical links

Conjugate priors update by adding sufficient statistics. Beta-Bernoulli, gamma-Poisson, Dirichlet-multinomial, and normal-normal models are the cleanest examples. In GLMs, canonical links set the linear predictor equal to the natural parameter.

Controls

Change prior strength and observed sufficient statistics. The posterior updates by simple addition.
