Differentiability

The error after subtracting a line is smaller than linear.

Derivative. The number \(f'(a)\) is the unique slope \(m\) such that \(f(a+h)-f(a)-mh=o(|h|)\).
Affine model. The tangent approximation is \(A_a(x)=f(a)+f'(a)(x-a)\).
Higher dimensions. Replace \(f'(a)h\) by a linear map \(Df(a)h\): \(f(a+h)=f(a)+Df(a)h+o(\|h\|)\).
