Exam summary

Define sₙ = sup{xₖ:k≥n}; then sₙ decreases.
Define iₙ = inf{xₖ:k≥n}; then iₙ increases.
limsup xₙ = infₙ sₙ and liminf xₙ = supₙ iₙ.
xₙ converges ⇔ limsup xₙ = liminf xₙ.
