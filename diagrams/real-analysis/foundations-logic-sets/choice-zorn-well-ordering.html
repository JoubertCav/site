Compact exam-style summary

Minimal statements and proof routes.

Statements over ZF

  • AC: every family of nonempty sets admits a choice function.
  • Zorn: if every chain in a nonempty poset has an upper bound, then the poset has a maximal element.
  • WO: every set admits a well-order.
  • Equivalence: AC ⇔ Zorn ⇔ WO.

Proof skeleton

  • WO⇒AC: choose the least element of each nonempty set.
  • AC⇒Zorn: use AC to get a maximal chain; its upper bound is maximal.
  • Zorn⇒WO: maximize a well-ordering of a subset; append any missing point.
  • Zorn⇒AC: maximize partial choice functions ordered by extension.
