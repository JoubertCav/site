ε–δ continuity

A point is continuous when every sufficiently small input-neighborhood around a is carried entirely inside a prescribed output-neighborhood around f(a).

The neighborhood picture

Move ε and δ. The δ-window in the domain is tested against the ε-band in the range.

δ-neighborhood ε-band allowed image escape
f continuous at a ⇔ ∀ε>0, ∃δ>0 : |x−a|<δ ⇒ |f(x)−f(a)|<ε.

What to notice

  • ε is chosen first: it is the required vertical accuracy.
  • δ is the response: it controls how close x must be to a.
  • Continuity means some δ works for every ε>0.

Sequential lens

Continuity also means every sequence xₙ→a is carried to f(xₙ)→f(a).

Symbolic equivalence

f continuous at a ⇔ xₙ→a ⇒ f(xₙ)→f(a).
